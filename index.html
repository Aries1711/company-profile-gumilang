    Vision

    • Develop and promote trade in the agricultural sector and positively impact the Indonesian economy.
    • to create mutually supportive cooperation between suppliers, producers, and consumers.

    is a company founded in Situbondo, East Java, Indonesia. Our company focuses on agricultural products. The company saw strong demand from the overseas market, such as coconut fiber, coconut shell, coconut broomstick, ginger, Tumeric, and tobacco.
