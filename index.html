    PT. Gumilang Tani Bersama

    "the world in your hand"

    Visi

    • Develop and promote trade in the agricultural sector and positively impact the Indonesian economy.
    • to create mutually supportive cooperation between suppliers, producers, and consumers.

    is a company founded in Situbondo, East Java, Indonesia. Our company focuses on agricultural products. The company saw strong demand from the overseas market, such as coconut fiber, coconut shell, coconut broomstick, ginger, Tumeric, and tobacco. We, together with farmers, will offer products that the world needs with high-quality processed without adding chemicals and environmentally friendly.

    Misi

    • Produce good quality products to compete in the international market
    • Committed to improving the level of service for consumers continuously
